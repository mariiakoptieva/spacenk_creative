The idea, in one image: the neighbourhood store as the hero of the campaign
The idea in 30 seconds

People trust a recommendation from someone who shops where they shop — but beauty retail speaks to every postcode with one national voice. So: each month, one Space NK store becomes the Neighbourhood Edit. A local curator — a micro-influencer, a store expert, or a loyal N.dulge customer — picks their five essentials. Their shelf goes in-store, their story goes on every channel, their neighbourhood gets invited in. Twelve months, twelve neighbourhoods.

One idea · four rotations
ROTATION 01
Local Marketing
  • Curator's five-product shelf in-store, with "why I love this" notes
  • Launch evening hosted by the curator — the event format I've delivered at activations
ROTATION 02
Social Media
  • Curator reels & carousels: shelf tour, "5 products, 1 neighbourhood"
  • UGC: locals post their top five with the hashtag to become the next curator
ROTATION 03
Email Marketing
  • N.dulge members near the store get the launch invite
  • Everyone else gets the editorial version, driving online sales of the five
ROTATION 04
Paid Media
  • Geo-targeted ads in a tight radius around the store, using curator content
  • Retargeting engaged users with the event invite — small budget, tightly measured
